# cool find
This is single (translated: BLOOD - INVIOLABLE) is by the former guitarist/vocalist of SEAPOOL, Suzuka. She has another project under Gigaevil Xarigani, but I believe she's focusing on this (for now). 

I absolutely love how experimental this is, I haven't heard anything like this before. 

It feels raw and emotional. She's really good at creating a dreadful atmosphere that is chaotic, esoteric, and hopeless.
