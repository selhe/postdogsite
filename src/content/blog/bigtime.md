# southlondon postpunk duo
very raw and simple production with drums vox and bass.
bass tone sounds nice and punchy.

drumming is really good over the whole thing, reminds me of maybe a more normal still houseplants, still very different.

the singing is also pretty emotional, i like when hes talking to his tesco meal on 'deal'.  bluesy bas stuff over the album that works nice and is groovy.

<b>fav song:</b> somerstown, outro is cool and he plays the nine chord.
deal, lyrics are cool, and he just plays 5 notes then goes AhhhhhhhhH
